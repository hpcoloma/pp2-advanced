Terminology

  • Gross Pay

    - is the employee's pay of any kind

  • Taxable Pay

    - is your gross pay, less any contributions you make.

  • Income Tax

    - is a levy imposed by the government on the money you earn, whether you're an individual or a business

  • Tax Credits

    - reduce the amount of Income Tax that you pay

  • Universal Social Charge (USC)

    - is a tax that has replaced both the income levy and the health levy (also known as the health contribution) since 1 January 2011. You pay the USC if your gross income is more than â‚¬13,000 per year.

  • Pay Related Social Income (PRSI)

    - contributions are paid into the Social Insurance Fund (SIF) which helps pay for benefits and pensions.

  • Pension Contribution

    - defined contribution plan.
